# Books & long-form reading

Mix **testing craft**, **systems/reliability**, and **leadership**—Staff QE and Quality Manager roles all benefit from fluency in how organizations decide what “good enough” means.

## Testing & quality practice

- *Explore It!* — Elisabeth Hendrickson — exploratory testing, charters, practical mindset.
- *Agile Testing* / *More Agile Testing* — Lisa Crispin & Janet Gregory — whole-team quality in agile delivery.
- *Perfect Software: And Other Illusions about Testing* — Gerald Weinberg — short essays, good for reframing expectations.

## Systems, reliability, and “quality at scale”

- *Site Reliability Engineering* (Google) — free online: https://sre.google/sre-book/table-of-contents/
- *The DevOps Handbook* — Kim, Humble, et al. — flow, feedback, continual learning.

## Leadership, org design, and management (Quality Manager–leaning)

- *The Manager’s Path* — Camille Fournier — engineering management progression; useful even for Staff ICs who lead without reports.
- *An Elegant Puzzle* — Will Larson — systems thinking applied to engineering orgs and leadership.
- *Accelerate* — Forsgren, Humble, Kim — metrics tied to delivery and stability (interpret carefully; avoid metric dysfunction).

## Staff IC–leaning (influence, scope, writing)

- *Staff Engineer* — Will Larson — paths and expectations for Staff+ ICs (adapt mentally from “generic Staff” to quality domain).
