# Certifications

Certifications are **optional** signals. Some employers value them; others care more about demonstrated work. Use this section to track what exists and whether it fits your market.

## Widely recognized in testing

| Name | Notes |
|------|--------|
| **ISTQB** (various levels / specialties) | Common in Europe and some global enterprises; syllabus-driven. Official: https://www.istqb.org/ |
| **ASTQB** | ISTQB exam administration in the United States: https://astqb.org/ |

## Adjacent domains (often relevant to QE careers)

- **AWS / Azure / GCP** certifications — if your role includes infra or cloud-heavy validation.
- **Security** — e.g., OWASP-aligned training (certification value varies by role).

## Manager-adjacent (optional; market-dependent)

Some Quality / Engineering Managers pursue **project management** or **agile facilitator** credentials (e.g., PMP, PMI-ACP, Scrum.org / Scrum Alliance paths). Value varies widely by company and region—track what **your** industry and target employers signal.
